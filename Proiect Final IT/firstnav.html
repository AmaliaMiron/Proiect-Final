A picture paints a thousand words

A logo is a visual representation of your brand, so why tell people what you do if you could just show them?

Visual puns are a designer’s best friend too – here Frost Bites have created a quirky ‘bite’ to show that they are a food company.

This is really easily achieved by overlapping two circles that are the same color as the background.

Use simple icons to communicate who you are and what you want to express. This way it is easier to be well known by the others.
