Color is key for good logo design

Monochromatic doesn’t always mean black and white! Sometimes black and white can seem harsh on our eyes, especially if we’re trying to create a feeling of zen.

You can use various shades of the same color to create subtle contrasts within your logo.By using various shades of pink, Serenity has created a logo that is very calming – much like their spa must be.

If you’ve been searching for the answer to, “what is good design?” well now you know.
